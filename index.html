Eyes On the Cattle

In today’s fast-paced world, traffic congestion caused by unexpected Road obstacles can lead to significant delays and inconvenience for computers. To address this issue, we have developed a practice solution using computer vision and machine learning.

Traffic congestion is a common problem on roads, often caused by unexpected obstacles such as wandering cattle. These situations not only disrupt traffic flow but also pose a danger to both drivers and the animals themselves. To mitigate these issues, we’ve designed a cattle detection system that identifies cattle on roads and alerts relevant authorities, allowing them to take immediate action. This system utilizes the power of YOLOv8 for real-time object detection and Ultralights for enhanced model handling.

We've designed a cattle detection system that: 1. Identifies Cattle: Using YOLOv8, an advanced object detection model, our system detects cattle on roads in real-time. 2. Alerts Authorities: When cattle are detected within a designated area (defined by a polygon), the system sends alerts to relevant authorities. 3. Prevents Jams: By proactively notifying authorities, we prevent potential traffic disruptions caused by cattle.
